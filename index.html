What We Offer

The Pampered Pooch Wash

A refreshing bath, blow-dry, brush-out, ear cleaning, and nail trim. Perfect for maintaining cleanliness between full grooms.

Full Spa Day Groom

Includes everything in the Pampered Pooch Wash plus a breed-specific haircut or full body trim. Your dog will leave looking and feeling their absolute best.

De-Shedding Treatment

Specialized shampoo and conditioning to reduce shedding, followed by thorough brushing to remove loose undercoat. Keeps your home cleaner and your dog more comfortable.
